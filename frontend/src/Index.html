ISLA Waterpark

Isla waterpark are one of the best waterparks in Bali. Built on an area of ​​28,000 square meters and located at Jalan Cocoa I No. Br Kedewatan, Ubud District, Badung Regency, Bali. Isla Waterpark has various water rides with high security systems and internationally certified lifeguards. Some of the rides that are widely known to the public include the tsunami waves with the highest waves in Indonesia, the Jet Coaster water slide with the tallest tower in Indonesia, as well as a stream pool with 8 different themes.
